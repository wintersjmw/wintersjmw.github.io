About Me

I was born in Chesapeake, VA in 1997 and have lived in Rhode Island and North Carolina as well. I love to build, design, watch, appreciate, and make art. In any form. I play drumset, guitar, and marimba and have always drawn for fun. These are things you can expect to see articles about as well as movie reviews! I love movies. A cliche thing to say at the very least but I like to believe I have watched and written about enough movies to be serious about it. Currently all of my movie reviews are stored in the archives of this website so feel free to check them out. They are from the time that I spent as a DJ and Movie Reviewer at WKNC in Raleigh, NC. A few of my favorites are What We Do in The Shadows, John Wick, and Drive. Check them out and let me know what you think in the comments.
